Lil Dicky is a comedian rapper hailing from an upper middle class neighborhood in Philidelphia. He has released one mixtape, one studio album, one e.p., and five singles. Lil Dicky began his career in 2011 and would release his finished mixtape in 2013. Lil Dicky first gained wide-spread fame with the release of the music video for his song "Ex-Boyfriend".
